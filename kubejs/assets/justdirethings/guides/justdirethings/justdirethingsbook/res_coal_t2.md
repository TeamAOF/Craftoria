# Blaze Ember

Blaze Ember is crafted by placing a block of [Primal Coal](./res_coal_t1.md) next to a [Blazebloom Goo](./gooblock_tier2.md). It burns hotter and more efficiently than its predecessor, generating even more RF/T.
